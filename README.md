# KEYSER-Intelligence


## Overview

Keyser Intelligence is an AI-powered platform that helps businesses understand data, track market changes, and make better decisions. It goes beyond traditional research by combining internal data with external signals like competitors, trends, and customer sentiment.

---

## Features

* **Sentiment Analysis** – Understand customer opinions from online sources
* **Competitor Tracking** – Monitor competitor strategies and changes
* **Predictive Analytics** – Forecast trends and future performance
* **Cross-Study Insights** – Connect findings across multiple studies
* **Market & Economic Context** – Analyze data with real-world factors
* **Business Impact Analysis** – Link insights to revenue and growth

---

## Tech Stack

* Frontend: React
* Backend: Node.js / FastAPI
* AI/ML: Python, NLP models, forecasting models
* Database: PostgreSQL / MongoDB

---

## Use Cases

* Market research
* Product strategy
* Competitive analysis
* Trend prediction

---

## Conclusion

Keyser Intelligence helps users see beyond data and make smarter, faster, and more informed decisions.
